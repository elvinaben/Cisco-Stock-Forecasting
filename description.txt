Description of the database in more detail

Date: 
	this is the day on which the information was revealed
	format AAAA-MM-JJ
	
Open:
	It is the value of the share at the opening of the stock market.

Close:
	This is the value of the stock at the close of trading

High:
	It is the highest value of the action during a day		

Low:
	It is the smallest value of the action during a day

Volume :

	This is the number of shares in the stock market
